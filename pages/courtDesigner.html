Design a Basketball Court

Let's design a court! Below, you will see options for background color, line color, court color, and more. After selecting these options, click the finished button to preview your design!

Court Options

Background Color

This color will be used as the surrounding color outside of the actual court, making it the likely first part of any design a spectator will notice. Feel free to make it vibrant or muted, any color will work beautifully.

Line Color

This color will be used as the color throughout the court. As courts are typically lighter wood colors, we recommend a darker or contrasting color to the base court color.

Court Color

This color will be used as the base color of the actual court, making it a key aspect of the design. Will your court respect tradition or blaze a new trail?

Center Logo

This logo will stamp the brand of the team on top of the court, marking this as the home of your team. Here is where magic is made. Memories of this hallowed ground will last for centuries. This is our home.

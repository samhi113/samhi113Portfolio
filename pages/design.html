Graphic Design Projects

SCIPS Logo

Our department merged with another department on campus and therefore changed names to convey our combined mission. Once the name was finalized and began seeing public usage, I was asked to design a new logo in a similar vein to our old logo. For this project, I utilized Adobe Illustrator for its easy ability to create various vector graphics. I recreated our old logo and then began changing these elements to encompass our new name and University Brand Guidelines. After proposing several iterations to department heads, one was chosen as a lead candidate, then alterations were made to improve legibility, and then contrasting versions for usage against a dark background.
